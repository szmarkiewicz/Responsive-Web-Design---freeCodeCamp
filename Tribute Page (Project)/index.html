Iga Świątek

A Polish professional tennis player

Here are a few facts about Iga.

  • Iga is currently ranked world No. 1 by the Women's Tennis Association (WTA).
  • She is the first player representing Poland to win a major singles title.
  • Świątek was the champion at the French Open in 2020 and 2022.
  • In 2020, she became the youngest singles champion at the French Open since Rafael Nadal in 2005 and the youngest women's singles champion at a major since Maria Sharapova in 2004.
  • With the subsequent retirement of world No. 1 Ashleigh Barty, she claimed the top ranking on 4 April 2022.
  • She also won another WTA 1000 title in Miami, becoming the fourth woman to win the Sunshine Double ▶.
  • Świątek is a cat lover and owns a black female cat named Grappa.

The Sunshine Double is a feat in tennis achieved when a player wins the titles of the Indian Wells Masters and the Miami Masters back-to-back.
